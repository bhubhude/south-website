Ngakho ibandla le MRP uma liba semandleni lizaqinisekisa ukuba lezo zimayini aziwondli abantu bokuza kodwa zizohlomulisa umphakathi njalo ibandla ngeke livume ukuthi umuntu ongayena wendawo asebenze kuleyo migodi.Njalo lezozimayini intela yazo izokwakha imigwaqo lokugebha amadamu ukuze izfuyo zithole amanzi Ibandla le MRP lizoqinisekisa ukuba imali yemithelo isebenzela abantu bendawo njalo kayiphumeli phandle kwe Tsholotsho. Ibandla le MRP lizavuselela ezamasiko isibonelo ukusokwa kwamajaha lo siko lokuthomba kwezintombi njalo lemfundiso ezafundisa amajaha ukuba uma eganwa yini okumele ayenze noma angamele ayenze kanjalo lezintombi ukuba umazigana yikuphi okulindeleke kuzo njalo yikuphi ezingamele zikwenze.Ngoba sinanzelele ukuthi siyisizwe imizi ayisaqini ngoba inengi lingena emshadweni lingafundisiwe.Njalo ibanndla ngeke livumele abaholi bomdabu bazihlanganise lezombusazwe. Ibandla le MRP lizakwenza ama project ukuthi abantu beziphilise njalo lizobheka ukuthi indawo ethile kumele ibe layiphi i project. Isibonelo indawo ezilamanzi amadamu lemithombo ezifana labo Dikili iqembu lizakhuthaza ukuba kuthengwe umishini edonsa amanzi ukuzela ukuthi kube lama Irrigation systems ukuba abantu belime, Ibandla lizakhuthaza ukuba ngeWard nge Ward kube lemishi yokuthunga ukwenzela ukuthi labo abaswelayo njalo sebebadala okanye izintandane bethungelwe impahla.Kulawo maProject ibandla izovumela ukuthi abantu abakwazi ukupheka utshwala be Rejestwe ukuze labo abanatha utshwala beyethanga kubo lokho kuyabe kuyikuvula amathuba omsebenzi.Ngakho ibandla ngeke lisayivumela ingwebu isebenze eTsholotsho ngoba uzulu kahlomuli lutho kuNgwebu kodwa uthenga utshwala imihla lamalanga leyomali ibisihuqhuluzwa ngamashona.
